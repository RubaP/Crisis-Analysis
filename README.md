# CrisisAnalysis
### Public understanding of the energy crisis and climate change: A comparative study of mainstream and social media debates

This project aims to investigate the public discourse around the energy crisis, in order to identify how these pivotal and contradictory needs are reconciled in this debate, what social actors
are held responsible and what current and future actions are predicated of them and why.
